Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

Summary: Velochat has no accounts and collects no personal information such as your name, email, or phone number. Your identity is a cryptographic inbox ID generated and stored on your own device. Messages are end-to-end encrypted using the XMTP protocol. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Velochat ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application on iOS. Velochat is a decentralized messaging app built on the XMTP protocol, allowing users to exchange direct and group messages without creating a traditional account.

By using Velochat, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Velochat is designed to collect as little information as possible:

  • Decentralized Identity: On first launch, the app automatically generates an XMTP inbox ID and wallet address on your device. This identity is not linked to your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Messages: Text, photo, and voice messages are transmitted using the XMTP protocol's end-to-end encryption. We do not operate a server that stores the plaintext content of your conversations.
  • Push Notification Token: If you enable notifications, your device's push token is registered with the XMTP push notification service so you can be notified of new messages. We do not read message content to generate these notifications.
  • Local Preferences: Contact notes, group nicknames, your chosen appearance (Light/Dark/System), and onboarding state are stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking data
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking

3. Camera and Microphone Permissions

Velochat requests access to your device camera solely to scan QR codes when adding a contact. The camera is never used to capture or store photos in the background.

Velochat requests access to your device microphone solely to record voice messages that you explicitly choose to send. Recordings are only created while you are actively recording, and are sent as an encrypted voice message; we do not record audio at any other time.

You can revoke either permission at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Velochat.

4. No Accounts, No Sign-In

Velochat does not use email, phone number, or social sign-in (such as Google or Apple Sign-In). Your decentralized identity is created automatically and is stored securely in your device's Keychain. There is no password to create, store, or recover.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your client keys and identity information are stored using iOS Keychain encryption. Message content is protected end-to-end using the XMTP network's security protocol, which provides forward secrecy so that messages cannot be retroactively decrypted even if a key is later compromised.

Because there is no central Velochat account database, there is no personal profile for us to delete on request. Uninstalling the app removes your locally stored identity and preferences from that device.

6. Third-Party Services

Velochat connects to the open-source, decentralized XMTP network to send and receive messages. Use of the XMTP network is governed by the XMTP protocol's own documentation, available at xmtp.org.

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

Velochat is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app collects no personal information from any user, no child-specific data is collected.

8. Your Rights

Since Velochat does not maintain a central account or profile for you, most of your data already lives only on your device under your control. If you have questions about your data or this policy, please contact us at daibou007@gmail.com.
