How to Use Velochat

1. Getting Started

Step 1 - Open Velochat. There is no sign-in screen; the app automatically creates your decentralized identity on first launch.
Step 2 - Once initialization finishes, you will see your Chats list, which is empty until you add a contact or join a group.
Your identity is generated once and stored securely on your device. You will not be asked to sign in again.

2. Adding a Contact

Step 1 - Tap the person icon in the top-left of the Chats screen to open "Me" and view your personal QR code.
Step 2 - Ask your contact to open Velochat and tap the "+" menu on their Chats screen, then "Add Contact".
Step 3 - Have them scan your QR code (or you scan theirs). A direct conversation is created automatically.

3. Starting a Group Chat

Step 1 - Tap the "+" menu on the Chats screen and choose "Create Group".
Step 2 - Select members from your existing contacts, then tap "Next".
Step 3 - Enter a group name and tap "Create". The group appears in your Chats list immediately.

4. Sending Messages

Step 1 - Open any conversation and type in the message field at the bottom.
Step 2 - Tap the "+" icon to attach a photo from your library or camera.
Step 3 - Tap the microphone icon to record a voice message; tap "Done" to send it or "Cancel" to discard it.

5. Appearance (Light / Dark / System)

Step 1 - Tap the person icon (Me), then the gear icon (Settings).
Step 2 - Under Appearance, choose Light, Dark, or System. The change applies immediately.

6. Notifications

Step 1 - Allow notifications when prompted to be alerted about new messages.
Step 2 - You can manage notification permission anytime from Settings, or directly in iOS Settings > Notifications > Velochat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app ask for camera permission?

Velochat uses the camera exclusively to scan QR codes when you add a contact. No photos are captured or stored unless you explicitly choose to send one in a chat.

Why does the app ask for microphone permission?

The microphone is used only while you are actively recording a voice message to send. Audio is never recorded in the background.

Do I need an account to use Velochat?

No. Velochat has no accounts, no email or phone sign-up, and no passwords. Your identity is created automatically and stored on your device.

How do I delete my data?

Because Velochat does not store your data on a central server, deleting your data is as simple as uninstalling the app, which removes your locally stored identity, keys, and preferences from that device. If you have questions about any data associated with your use of the app, email daibou007@gmail.com.

I can't hear voice messages, or playback sounds wrong.

Try these steps in order:

  1. Make sure the device is not in silent/mute mode (use the side switch or Control Center).
  2. Hold the phone to your ear during playback to switch to the earpiece, or move it away to use the speaker.
  3. Force-quit the app and relaunch.

If the problem persists, email us at daibou007@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version.

The app crashed or something isn't working.

Force-quit and relaunch the app. If the problem persists, email us at daibou007@gmail.com with a description of what happened, your device model, and iOS version.
